UML Class, Activity, and Sequence Diagrams

I built these diagrams using Lucidchart (www.lucidchart.com). SmartDraw symbols are slightly different than most of the UML references I have found (e.g., SmartDraw uses filled arrows to show inheritance, though most UML resources indicate that this should be an empty/hollow arrow). Lucidchart also gives users more control over where and how lines intersect, which made it easier for me to design UML diagrams later in the class.
