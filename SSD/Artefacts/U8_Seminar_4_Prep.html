Why did you select the algorithm you chose?

HS256 is a symmetric encryption algorithm known for its speed and efficiency, whilst simultaneously providing ample security through the SHA-256 hash algorithm (Johnson, 2022).

Would it meet the GDPR regulations? Justify your answer.

HS256 satisfies GDPR requirements based on the fact that GDPR Chapter 4, Section 2, Article 32 does prescribe specific standards for encryption – only that personal data must be encrypted, and that system owners must assess the appropriate level of security proportional to the risks of data compromise (European Union, 2016; Proton AG, 2019).
